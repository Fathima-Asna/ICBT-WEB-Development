Privacy Policy

Learn how GlobeTrek Adventures collects, manages, and safeguards your explorer profile data.

1. Information Collection

We collect details you provide voluntarily when creating accounts, searching for accommodations, booking curated tour packages, or submitting contact requests. This information typically includes your name, email address, password hash, and booking parameters.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is processed strictly to fulfill your booking reservations, authorize secure session access, check for duplicate logins, validate security tokens, and communicate details regarding your selected tour packages.

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement state-of-the-art security checks. Database connections leverage prepared statements to block SQL injections. We employ cryptographic hashing mechanisms to protect stored passwords. Web sessions use secure state tokens to block concurrent logins.

4. Cookies and Sessions

We use lightweight cookies strictly to store PHP session identification tokens. These cookies do not track cross-site behavioral patterns and are used exclusively to maintain your authentication state as you navigate between inner pages.

5. Sharing of Information

GlobeTrek Adventures will never lease, trade, or distribute your email or personal registration records to external tracking networks or advertising agencies.
